About Me

Greetings!

I am currently a senior at Brooklyn College who will be graduating in December 2019 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science. I consider myself a lifelong learner who is tolerant and opened to listening to new ideas. I enjoy tinkering with code because there is always room for improvement, but I also have a passion for the learning process and engaging others in the thrill of problem solving. My goal in life is to support my family, to advise and guide them, my peers and the next generation, and to "keep moving forward," as said in Meet the Robinsons.


Interests

Outside of programming, I have many hobbies. I enjoy listening to music and participating in karaoke. I enjoy biking as a form of commuting, exercise, thrifting, and promoting a greener environment. Long scenic rides are the best! I love playing volleyball, handball and going on adventures with friends. I've found the greatest peace in PC gaming. I also enjoy finding ways to organize and automate my workload in an effort towards efficiency.
